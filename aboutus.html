About Us




Who are us?

The site focuses on the ancient to modern history of the Middle East and the Mediterranean world, and on the early modern and modern history of Asia and Europe. Byzantine, medieval Anatolian, Ottoman and Asian history are major topics of teaching and active research. Our aim is making people conscious about the history subjects they are interested in.
